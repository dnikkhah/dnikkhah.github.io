About Me

I'm a communications professional with 15 years' experience in content, marketing, branding and pr. With a foundation in print and broadcast journalism, I have helped businesses optimize their brand, image and reach. Most recently I helped build an educational technology company, serving as Director of Communications and COO. In those roles I managed business strategy, strategic partnerships, sales, digital marketing, social media strategy and content, press and promotional events.

My prior experience includes creating marketing at a hedge fund and a municipal bond firm, running a media training program for executives, event coordination, writing/shooting/editing promotional videos, photography, writing articles and press releases, television news production and filming. While at NY1 News, ABC News and Inside Edition, I produced news segments, was a field reporter and story coordinator.

I am graduate of Barnard College and Columbia University Journalism School.
